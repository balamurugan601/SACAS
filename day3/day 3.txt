Sales & Customer Analysis Report (April 2023)
The analysis highlights key patterns in customer behavior, payment preferences, and restaurant performance.
1. Sales by Member Type & Payment Method


Gold members contribute significantly higher order quantities than Regular members.


UPI is the most preferred payment method across both member types, followed by COD, while Card payments are the least used.


Gold members show stronger engagement across all payment methods.


2. Customer Contribution


Sales are distributed across many customers, with a few customers (e.g., Rituraj Desai, Kartik Patel) contributing a relatively higher share.


The customer base is diversified, indicating low dependency on a single customer.


3. Restaurant Performance


Multiple restaurant codes show consistent order counts across months.


No extreme fluctuations are observed, suggesting stable demand across restaurants.


April sales align closely with previous months, indicating steady business performance.


Overall Insight
The business benefits strongly from Gold membership users and digital payments (UPI). Customer demand is well-distributed, and restaurant performance remains stable, reflecting healthy operational consistency.
